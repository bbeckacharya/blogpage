The last day when i saw myself ;
Its been a long that i haven't written any articles. So unofficially in roman language i am going to share a short moment here. 

Tyo din ma bistara bata uthna khojey tara kasto garungo lagyo. Herey afai lai ma tw utheko xu tara jyan bistara mei xa. Feri sarir ma gayera uthna khojey.. Aha sakina… karaye, chichhaye, jhakjhakye afai lai… tara sunidine kasle. Ma ta laas banisakexu. Ekxin ma aama aaunu vayo cup ma doodh ko chiya liyera uthauna. Mero sarir ko nadi ma samatera uthauna khojnu vayo tara ma ghamandi kaha uthey rw. Aama ko haat bata chiya ko cup vui ma khasyo, runu vayo malai bolaunu vayo. Ma boliraheko xu.. Sabai dekhdai xu tara sunidine kasle. Tyo sisa ko cup sangai meri aama le mero lagi  buneko sapana chaknachur vayo.Mero chiso sarir ma haat rakhdai chichhaunu vayo. Ane baba aaunu vayo dhoka vitra.. Stabda hunu vayo. Tyo man ma kasle pasera herna sakxa ra k bitiraheko xa vanne kura jun uha le afno khun ko chiso laas agadi paltiraheko dekhda thamera addinu vayeko thiyo. Mero baini tw dhoka mai ladi na tw ma uthauna jana nai sakey. 
Ma santa aatma banera kotha ko kuna ma basera sabai herirahe.. na kei garna nai sakthe. Samaya kati xito gayo. Baira natedar haru le baas ko varyang banaisakexan. Hijo sangai ghar farkida kadh ma haat rakheka mathi ka dai le ma mareko sankha fukdai thiye. 
Mera sathi haru le na bolna sakey na tw aasu thamna nai sabai stabda thiye. Kati swarthi raixan ti malai dusman manne haru, sarir chiso laas huda pane thikai vayo vanera gui gui kura garna xodenan. Mero baba ko kadh ma mero laas ko varyang pareko dekhda pane ma sithil nai rahe. 
Bato ma seto dhan ko fula, abir rw sikka paisa xardai malai khola ma lagiyo. Sabai aaye mero sarir poliyo ane ma bistarai bistarai mathi mathi lop hudai gaye.
1-2 din gardai sabai ko messenger ko chatbox bata tala tala sardai gaye. Sathi haru lai 1-2 din abhav mahasus vayo tara pane bistarai birsidai gaye. Mera aama baba tw sadhai sok ma nai rahirahe. 
Yesari mahakabi laxmiprasad le vanejhai ma sunya thiye, sunya mei bilaigaye.
